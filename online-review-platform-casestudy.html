Online review platform Case Study

Online review platform where users can review and rate SWISS construction companies they have used in the past. These reviews and ratings will be available for future users who are searching to hire a construction company to make the best choice.

Technologies: Python, Django, Reactjs, Docker

Industry: Real Estate & Travel

Contact Us

Challenges

Anyone will be able to come on the online review platform and search for a company X and they will be able to see all the reviews and ratings from other users who have worked with that X company.

If users are planning to do some construction work around their house and they do not know a company in field they need, they can come on the online review platform and do a research by field for example they can search “painters”, “electrician”, “architect” and we will give them the top 3 best rated and reviewed companies in their area and the contact information of these top 3 companies.

  • The landing page of the web app will have a search tool bar of construction companies, either by name, by location or by area of work.
  • The login section to profile for the people who wants to review and rate a company with the possibility to take and upload pictures with the web app directly.
  • The review and rating section similar to the one from the website platform.
  • We see the ratings and reviews given to construction companies. Develop a quote section in this platform.
  • The login section for construction companies to be able to make membership payments and see the referrals we have given to them for quotes.
  • Team presentation, Contact us (chat possibility during working hours) and Videos (explaining how our platform work)
  • The web app is free to use by users who are searching reviews and ratings or who want to write a review and rating.
